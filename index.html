Our Values and Principles

Our values are what we stand for. They are our compass that guides us to the right direction in the sea of chaos. We believe in our values deeply and make sacrifices to pursue them.

Be Open
Our contributions at Dorkodu to reach our goals, is open for everyone. To gain the trust and movement of the community, we share our experiences.
Create Opportunity
We believe that open source can create a big opportunity for everyone. This is the best for both us and the community.
Create Value
If you do something that you really love, the outcome will be valuable. Believe in yourself and your work.
Give Freedom
Freedom is essential for being creative. Here at Dorkodu, everyone uses 20% of their time to create their own masterpieces.
